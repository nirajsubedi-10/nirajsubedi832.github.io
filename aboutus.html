Our History

Phidim Secondary School is the only community school that has succeeded in creating an outstanding history over the years in terms of education, infrastructure, economy, and management, situated in the center of the beautiful town of Phidim, in Phidim Municipality, Panchthar District. Initially established as Prasad Primary School in 1949 BS through the efforts of the late Prasad Singh Nembang, it was upgraded to Prasad Middle School in 1965 BS, Prasad High School in 1970 BS, Phidim High School in 1974 BS, Phidim Vocational Secondary School in 1979 BS, and is currently known as Phidim Secondary School. This school now conducts teaching activities from the Early Childhood Development Center to Grade 12 in both Nepali and English mediums. The overall development of this school has become a focal point of attraction for everyone. Today’s era is an era of information, technology, and global competition. Producing skilled manpower capable of competing in the global market is the current challenge. In community schools, equal access for the general public, accountable management, and good governance are made effective, and the maximum use of the latest information technology is promoted.
